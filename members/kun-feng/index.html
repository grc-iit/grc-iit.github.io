Dr. Kun Feng

Dr. Kun Feng is a Research Software Engineer in the Department of Computer Science at the Illinois Institute of Technology and a member of the Gnosis Research Center. Dr. Kun Feng works under the guidance of Dr. Xian-He Sun.
