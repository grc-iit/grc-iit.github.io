News

This page will be updated with the latest news and events from the center. For more timely updates, please follow us on X.

Aug 7, 2023Drs. Xian-He Sun and Anthony Kougkas have received a grant from the Department of Energy (DoE) for the project, "DTIO: Enabling Computational Storage using Data-Tasks and Asynchronous I/O."
Sep 1, 2022Drs. Xian-He Sun and Anthony Kougkas have received a grant from the Department of Energy (DoE) for the project, "Coeus: Accelerating Scientific Insights Using Enriched Metadata."
May, 2022Dr. Xian-He Sun recieved the Distinguished Alumni Award, College of Engineering, Michigan State University. Related IIT news article can be found here.
Nov 8, 2021The Hermes software, an NSF-funded joint effort between the Illinois Institute of Technology (IIT) and The HDF Group, is now in beta release.
May 11, 2021

Drs. Xian-He Sun and Anthony Kougkas have received a $4 million grant from the National Science Foundation (NSF) for the project, "ChronoLog: A High-Performance Storage Infrastructure for Activity and Log Workloads." The award is expected to be $4 million over four years. Illinois Tech was awarded $2.7 million of the grant and the University of Chicago was awarded $1.3 million of the grant.

May 10, 2021Hariharan Devarajan, Huihuo Zheng, Anthony Kougkas, Xian-He Sun, and Venkatram Vishwanath have received the best paper award in the 21st IEEE/ACM International Symposium on Cluster, Cloud and Internet Computing (CCGRID'21). Related HPCwire article can be found here.
Dec 16, 2020The Hermes software, an NSF-funded joint effort between the Illinois Tech and The HDF Group, has been launched! Related news can be found here.
Dec 16, 2020Dr. Xian-He Sun has been appointed as the Ron Hochsprung Endowed Chair in Computer Science.
Sep 30, 2020Drs. Rujia Wang, Kyle Hale, and Xian-He Sun have received a grant from the NSF to build a new computer architecture that will reduce the energy and time needed to process big data sets.
Jun 24, 2019Anthony Kougkas, Hariharan Devarajan, Jay Lofstead, and Xian-He Sun have received the Karsten Schwan Best Paper Award in the 28th International Symposium on High-Performance Parallel and Distributed Computing (HPDC'19). The paper can be found here.
Sep 12, 2018

Dr. Xian-He Sun has received a $3 Million NSF Framework Grant for the project, "Framework: Software: NSCI: Collaborative Research: Hermes: Extending the HDF Library to Support Intelligent I/O Buffering for Deep Memory and Storage Hierarchy Systems." The award is expected to be $3 million over four years. It is reported by The Center for Interdisciplinary Scientific Computation (CISC) here. More information of this project can be found here.

May 4, 2017

PortHadoop has received wide media coverage in supporting the merge of HPC and cloud computing. It has been reported by several media, including HPCwire, Phys.org, ENN, insideHPC, ProjectPro, and XinhuaNet.

Feb 27, 2017Dr. Xian-He Sun has received 2016 Golden Core Award from IEEE Computer Society.
Aug, 2016Dr. Xian-He Sun has received the IEEE Computer Society Meritorious Service Certificate from the IEEE Computer Society President Roger U. Fujii.
Aug 23, 2016Y. Chen, C. Chen, Y. Yin, X.-H. Sun, R. Thakur, and W. Gropp have received the Best Paper Award of the 14th IEEE International Symposium on Parallel and Distributed Processing with Applications (ISPA 2016). More information can be found here.
